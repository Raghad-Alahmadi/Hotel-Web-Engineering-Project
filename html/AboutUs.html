MAGNOLIA

In the 1950s, in the town of Riyadh, a Saudi family, raised five children with hearts full of kindness. father's act of helping those in need by providing food and shelter left a lasting impression on them. As the children grew, they decided to create something to honor their father's legacy. In 1972 They launched a "magnolia" The hotel, born from a shared desire to continue their father's mission, provided shelter and meals to anyone in need. The Magnolia Hotel became a symbol of hope and kindness in Saudi Arabia and many other countries. The siblings continued their father's mission of serving the community, offering not just lodging but a sense of belonging. Today, magnolia stands as a beacon of love and compassion, a testament to the enduring power of family values and a commitment to making the world a better place.
